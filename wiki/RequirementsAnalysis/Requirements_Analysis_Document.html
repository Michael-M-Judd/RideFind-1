The purpose of the requirements analysis document or RAD is to determine the needs and conditions to meet for our RideFind software.

In this document (accessed by following the links through Redmine), we outline the purpose and scope of our software as well as functional and non-functional requirements. Use cases and a scenario are outlined to demonstrate all core functionalities of the system. Mockups of the GUI interface as well as a description of how to navigate through the interface is also provided.

Finally we provide a link to our Gantt chart to outline our team's timeline over the next 7 weeks as well as team roles to ensure that we have a minimally viable product by week 12 of the course.
